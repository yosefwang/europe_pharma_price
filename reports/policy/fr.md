## `PRIX_FABRICANT_EUR` → **Manufacturer-side price**

- **Interpretation ID:** `927bbc42-d5f4-4361-946e-c47be73aa6c2`
- **Effective:** 2003-01-01 — present
- **Confidence:** high
- **VAT included:** False
- **Margin:** none (excludes wholesale and pharmacy margin)

### Reading

PRIX_FABRICANT_EUR (prix fabricant hors taxes, PFHT) is the regulated manufacturer ex-factory price excluding VAT, negotiated between the marketing authorisation holder and the Comité économique des produits de santé (CEPS) under the Code de la sécurité sociale. It is published as the gross convention price, before any confidential rebate.

### Source references

- Code de la sécurité sociale, Article L162-16-4 et seq.
- Accord-cadre CEPS / Les Entreprises du Médicament (LEEM)
- https://www.ameli.fr/medicaments-prix-fabricant

### Caveats

- Confidential CEPS rebates may apply; the published value reflects the gross convention price, not the net realised price.
- For products under référence aux comparateurs européens, this published value is itself influenced by reference pricing and should not be treated as independently observed.
