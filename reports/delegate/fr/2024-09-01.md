## Local field descriptions

### `PRIX_FABRICANT_EUR`

Manufacturer price (prix fabricant hors taxes, PFHT) in euros, as published in the Ameli base. This is the regulated price at which the marketing authorisation holder sells, before wholesale and pharmacy margins and before VAT.

### `CIP13`

Code Identifiant de Présentation à 13 chiffres — the French national product identifier, country-specific. Different from the EAN used in some other regimes.
