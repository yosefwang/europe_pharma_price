## Local field descriptions

### `Reimbursement Price`

Published in the PCRS druglist as 'Reimbursement Price'. Per S.I. No. 639/2019 Article 2, this column is the 'ingredient cost', defined as the ex-factory price plus a wholesale mark-up of 8% (or 12% for fridge items). It excludes VAT and excludes pharmacy mark-up and dispensing fees (paid separately under GMS/DPS/LTI). Maps to comparison category pharmacy_purchase_price.

### `Code`

PCRS-assigned national product code. Stable per pack but country-specific — does not interoperate with EAN/CIP codes.

### `INN`

International Nonproprietary Name as published by PCRS. Approximately 88% of rows in the May 2026 download carry an INN; missing INNs typically indicate combination products, dental items, or ostomy items not in scope.
